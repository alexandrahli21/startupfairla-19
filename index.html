About

UCLA's 7th annual Startup Fair LA aims to connect the best and brightest students to premier rising startups in the Los Angeles area. It is a great way for students of all majors to interact with companies, offering an excellent opportunity for them to find full-time positions and internships in a close-knit startup environment. We hope to see you there!

Startup Fair will also serve as an exciting opportunity for recruiters to spread the word about your companies and increase university presence. This is one of the few startup-exclusive events at UCLA, so take advantage of this chance to recruit some of the best talent in the area!
